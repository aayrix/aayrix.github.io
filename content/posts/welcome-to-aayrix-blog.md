Welcome to **Aayrix** — a blog focused on practical developer guides you can follow step by step. Every post includes real commands you can copy and run on your machine.

## What You'll Find Here

This site covers topics I use daily as a developer:

- **Linux** — Installing and configuring Arch Linux from scratch
- **GitHub** — Setting up Git, authenticating, and working with repositories
- **GitHub CLI** — Managing repos, issues, and PRs from the terminal
- **Hugo** — Building fast static sites and deploying to GitHub Pages

Each guide is written to be complete: prerequisites, every command, and a short explanation of what it does.
